Classic Homemade Lasagna

A family favorite Italian dish

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • 3 cups ricotta cheese
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 jars (24 oz each) marinara sauce
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  3. In a large skillet, brown ground beef and Italian sausage with garlic.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, salt, pepper, and fresh basil.
  5. Spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom of a 9×13 baking dish.
  6. Layer: noodles → ricotta mixture → meat → sauce → mozzarella.
  7. Repeat layers 2-3 times, ending with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  9. Remove foil and bake another 25 minutes until cheese is golden.
  10. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
